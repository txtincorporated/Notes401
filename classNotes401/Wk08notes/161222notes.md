#### TERMS & CONCEPTS

  * **`.factory()`:**  the service you can call to get the single instance
  * **`.value()`:**  just the single instance
  * **`.provider()`:**  wrapper you can put around `.factory` inside of `this.$get = <.factory() instance>`
